The Founding Pastors

LEADERSHIP

Rev. Ben Ndubueze

General Overseer

Reverend Ben Ndubueze is the founding Pastor of King Jesus Vineyard Deliverance Ministry aka King of Nations Chapel. He is ordained and anointed by our Lord Jesus Christ to cause upheavels in the kingdom of darkness in this end time spiritual dispensation. he has been doing this divine assignment with proven testimonies of sign and wonders trailing his ministry of reconciliation in the body of Christ. He is a Prophet, teacher and a preacher of the Word.

Rev. Tricia Ndubueze

Our amiable wife of the General Overseer of the Church, Rev. Mrs. Tricia Ndubueze is a Minister of the GOSPEL and SONGS in the Choir of the Church, which she nurtured from its cradle up till now. She has been a committed child of God from her infancy and has grown to become an ardent believer of Jesus Christ and a Defender of the True Faith. She is a very industrious woman who has strictly combined the domestic chores of her home and the onerous responsibility of assisting her husband profusely in the administration of the Church and Ministry.
